Welcome to AstroPie

Nestled in the heart of the city, AstroPie offers an authentic Italian dining experience like no other. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be transported to the vibrant streets of Italy, where good food and great company are at the center of life.

Our Story

Founded in 1998, our family-owned restaurant has always been a labor of love. Inspired by the recipes passed down through generations, we bring the rich culinary heritage of Italy to your plate. Every dish tells a story, and every meal is a celebration of flavor.

Our Philosophy

"The secret ingredient is always love."

At AstroPie, we believe in the simple joy of fresh ingredients, time-honored techniques, and a passion for creating memorable meals. From our hand-rolled pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, every bite is crafted with care.

What Makes Us Special?

  • Authenticity: Traditional recipes, just like Nonna used to make.
  • Fresh Ingredients: Locally sourced produce and imported Italian staples.
  • Warm Atmosphere: A place where everyone feels like family.

Join Us

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in the pleasures of Italian cuisine, AstroPie is the perfect destination. Come for the food, stay for the memories.
